About PurpleGG

Made by Purple — Gorilla Tag Player

PurpleGG started as a small side project: finding Murder Mystery 2 YouTubers with small followings and giving them fan art they might never have gotten otherwise. A lot of these creators have never had a single piece of fan art made for them — so when they get their first one here, it actually means something.

  • Find small MM2 content creators who deserve more attention.
  • Commission or collect their first-ever fan art.
  • Keep everything honest — if art isn't ready, the site says so instead of faking it.
  • Built and run by Purple, a Gorilla Tag player who also loves MM2.
